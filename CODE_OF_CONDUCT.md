# Code of Conduct / 社区行为准则

## Our commitment / 我们的承诺

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience level, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

我们致力于建设开放、友善、无骚扰的协作环境，不因年龄、身体状况、残障、族裔、性别认同与表达、经验、教育、经济状况、国籍、外貌、种族、宗教或性取向等因素区别对待参与者。

## Expected behavior / 倡导的行为

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive. 尊重他人，耐心且建设性地沟通。
- Give and accept technical feedback in good faith. 善意提出并接受技术反馈。
- Focus on the work and its impact, not personal characteristics. 聚焦工作与影响，不进行人身评价。
- Protect private thesis data and disclose conflicts of interest. 保护论文隐私，并披露利益冲突。
- Correct mistakes openly and help newcomers participate. 坦诚纠错，并帮助新贡献者参与。

## Unacceptable behavior / 不可接受的行为

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, trolling, sustained disruption, publishing another person's private information, or encouraging academic fraud are not acceptable in project spaces or when representing the project.

骚扰、威胁、歧视、性化言行、恶意挑衅、持续扰乱讨论、公开他人隐私或鼓励学术不端，均不可接受。

## Enforcement / 执行

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this policy, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Enforcement will consider context, impact, and a proportionate response.

维护者可删除违反本准则的内容，并视情节限制相关参与权限；处理时会综合考虑背景、影响与措施的适当性。

## Reporting / 举报

Use GitHub's private report/block features or contact the repository owner through a private channel shown on their GitHub profile. If no private channel is available, open a minimal issue labeled `moderation` without sensitive details and ask a maintainer to establish private contact. Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible; retaliation against a reporter is prohibited.

请使用 GitHub 的私密举报/屏蔽功能，或通过仓库所有者主页提供的私密渠道联系。若没有私密渠道，可提交不含敏感细节且标记为 `moderation` 的简短 Issue，请维护者建立私下联系。项目会在合理范围内保密处理，并禁止报复举报人。

This policy applies in all project spaces and whenever someone publicly represents the project.
